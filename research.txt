- Who am I?
	Jacob Moore, Junior Developer at Applianz. Love my cats, games (MtG and Fighting games), learning new things.

- Who is the audience?
	Java developers, folks interested in Java.

- Why are they coming?
	They're coming to a devICT event to hear an interesting talk. I am the main attraction.

- Will this be a keynote or more interactive?
	A keynote. I want to cover the background and initial startup of Kotlin as a story, then get into the implementation details. Little/no live code.
	
- What questions will the audience want answered in this talk?
	What is Kotlin?
	What is its origin story?
	What drove the creators to make it, when the JVM is so crowded already?
	Who is working on Kotlin?
	As a Java dev, why should I learn Kotlin?
	As an Android dev, why should I learn Kotlin?
	What does Kotlin do well?
	What doesn't it do well?
	How do I learn Kotlin?
